Service and Programs

Franklin County Family and Community Services offers a number of programs and services to individuals and the community. The programs range from child and adolescent services to residential programs for those currently in treatment. Please use the links below to navigate the services and programs we offer.

Children and Adolescents

Bright Changes

Bright Changes program is a comprehensive, youth guided, family driven and community supported mental health and substance abuse system of care.

Developmental Disabilities

CHOICES2 Innovative Community Services

CHOICES2 provides innovative community service programs ranging from employment to family support

Mental Health

Providing reputable mental health services for adults with behavioral, emotional, or substance-related disorders

Residential Services

The Residential Department provides housing and support services to adolescents and adults with mental health and/or substance abuse challenges.

Substance Abuse

The Cobb Recovery Center provides compassionate substance abuse and addictive disease treatment programs
