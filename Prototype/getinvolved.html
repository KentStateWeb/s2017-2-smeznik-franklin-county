Make a Difference, Change a Life

Volunteer

Each year, Franklin County relies on extraordinary individuals and groups to fulfill our mission. There are so many different ways to volunteer at Franklin County Family and Community Services such as knitting hats to mentoring a child.If you are interested in volunteering, please fill out the form below and one of our staff members will reach out to.

Other Ways to Get Involved

Organize/Participate in a Food Drive

Food drives help the Franklin County Pantry serve the more than 600 households each and every month who come to us in need. Food drives can be sponsored by individuals, corporations, clubs, schools and associations.

Attend an Event

From bidding on an auction item, attending a ridge dinner with friends and family, to bowling for kids, Franklin County Family & Community Services’ events offer great ways to support our efforts of “improving lives for a lifetime”.
